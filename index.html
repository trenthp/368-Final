About

The thing I love most about design & development is all of the different approaches you can take. Which angle is best when it comes to one problem or another? When it comes to design, the user experience is paramount. I am a UX designer at heart. A problem cannot simply be solved; it takes design and functionality to be worth anything. I am finishing as a student at Eastern Washington University studying Visual Communication Design. I am always looking forward to expanding my know-how on all design fronts. I'm all about water and pineapples. I am a super nice guy, and would enjoy collaborating on UX/web/graphic designs. Drop me a line and we'll chat.
