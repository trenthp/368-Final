About

The thing I love most about design is the variety of approaches you can take. Which angle is best when it comes to one problem or another? When it comes to design, the user experience is paramount (although business objectives can be important). I am a holistic designer at heart. A problem cannot simply be solved; it takes design and functionality to be worth anything. My process may change dependent on the project, but there is always the very necessary analysis of what is trying to be accomplished, research, and sketches to get me going.


Hello, my name is Trent Petersen & I am a holistic (UX/UI/Product) designer. My background is in Visual Communication Design. I am always looking forward to expanding my know-how on all design fronts. I'm all about water and pineapples. I am a super nice guy, and would enjoy collaborating on UX/web/graphic designs. Drop me a line and we'll chat.
