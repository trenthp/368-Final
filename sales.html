02 sales target app

In order for sales people to have the best idea to who they are trying to sell, leads should have plenty of information readily available.
This app was designed to get understandable information in front of the seller easily, and as a quick reference (for those on-the-fly encounters).

the brief

This client's needs were to collect data pulled from different sources and pool everything into an app that presented the sales person with all the information possible. This allows the seller to adapt their pitch in the best way possible.

*due to the nature of work, sometimes it is necessary to hide key information about clients

← about this screen
This page gives a breakdown of key segments and markets in which the target companies operates.

the approach

Collecting all of the types of information was important, so it started there. After I got a snapshot of everything that a sales person would need and be ble to get their hands on, then began the process of laying it all out to make cohesive groups.

The next big step was to make sure we had data management handled correctly. No one wants old and outdated data. Working closely with the developer, we hammered out a way to track the data coming in and dropping the old.

the result

An application that delivers key information in an effective way that helps the sales team do their job. The overall interface works well in its communication to the user.
