The Brief

RedRover is a unique offering for any startup who is lacking the proper funding needed to get their initial idea off the ground, or at least to a minimal viable product to be able to pitch their idea to investors. RedRover needed a very low key user interface to be able to accomplish its initial goals of getting different groups of designers, developers, business people, and the like in touch in order to facilitate a transfer of services in exchange for services.

The Approach

It was important to keep in mind a streamlined approach to the UX and UI; keep the app to as small a footprint as possible.

The Result

The bare minimum is introduced to the user in order to set up their profile, find new contacts that match for a proper exchange of services, and track those in their past to continue networking or exchanging services. The user is presented with a single page and a small number of possible subviews which provide options and allow for giving reviews to past exchanges.
