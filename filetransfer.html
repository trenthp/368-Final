The Brief

This project is a redesign of an existing system used mainly by a large auto manufacturer and their distributors. The app needed a refresh on its design, including for the user experience and user interface. It will be used by a larger group of people including the marketing teams, and eventually be opened up to more industries.

The Approach

To start, the entire existing app was broken down to the basics. The functionality was not to be changed, so it was important as we put the pieces back together that we didnâ€™t include additional functionality. By reworking the architecture of the sitemap and making the workflow make sense for the target user, I could get the lay all of the pieces in the correct places.

The Result

Placement of the existing features made the app have a workflow that made much more sense from the user. User testing conducted showed that new users were able to navigate the app with ease, and existing users were able to find their way without issues, even if they had been using the old app for years.
