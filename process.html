Overview

Designing an entire product takes a constant back and forth between looking at the forest and the trees. Throughout the project I'm diligently looking at the big picture, taking a deep dive into a particular feature, and then stepping back to look again at the big picture.

I offer my clients my attention & care throughout the entire process. I expect clients to provide me things I need to be successful, which includes proper communication throughout the engagement.
